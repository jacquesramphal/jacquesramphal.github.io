# Consulting Agreement (Master)

A basic master consulting agreement. This governs the relationship; the SOW governs each specific engagement. Get a lawyer to review this before using it for real.

**IMPORTANT: This is a starting draft, not legal advice. Have a Canadian or relevant-jurisdiction lawyer review before sending to a client.**

---

# Independent Contractor / Consulting Agreement

This Agreement is entered into as of [Date] ("Effective Date") between:

**Client:** [Company Legal Name], a [province/state] [corporation/LLC/etc.] ("Client")

**Consultant:** Jacques Ramphal, operating as [business name or "an independent contractor"] ("Consultant")

---

## 1. Services

Consultant will perform the services described in one or more Statements of Work (each, an "SOW") attached to or referencing this Agreement. Each SOW is incorporated into this Agreement by reference.

Consultant will perform the services as an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, or joint venture relationship.

---

## 2. Compensation

Client will pay Consultant the fees set out in each SOW. Invoices are due within 15 days of the invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month (18% annually).

Consultant may pause work if an invoice is more than 30 days past due, with written notice to Client.

---

## 3. Expenses

Client will reimburse pre-approved expenses within 30 days of submission with receipts. No expenses will be incurred without prior written approval.

---

## 4. Intellectual Property

**Client-specific deliverables:** Upon receipt of full payment for an SOW, Consultant assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in deliverables created specifically for Client under that SOW.

**Pre-existing and general IP:** Consultant retains ownership of all methodologies, frameworks, tools, code libraries, and templates that existed before the engagement or were developed independently of Client. Consultant grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual license to use any such materials incorporated into deliverables, solely to the extent necessary to use those deliverables.

**Portfolio rights:** Consultant may describe the engagement in general terms (without disclosing confidential information) in a portfolio, case study, or professional profile after the engagement concludes.

---

## 5. Confidentiality

Each party will keep confidential any proprietary or non-public information received from the other party and will not disclose it to third parties without prior written consent, except as required by law.

This obligation survives termination of the Agreement for 2 years.

Confidentiality does not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was already known to the receiving party; (c) is independently developed without use of confidential information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law or court order.

---

## 6. Non-Solicitation

During the term of this Agreement and for 12 months after, neither party will solicit the other party's employees or contractors for employment or engagement, without prior written consent.

---

## 7. Term and Termination

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated.

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination: (a) Client will pay for all work completed to the termination date; (b) Consultant will deliver all work in progress; (c) both parties will return or destroy the other's confidential information on request.

Client may terminate immediately for material breach by Consultant that is not cured within 10 business days of written notice.

---

## 8. Limitation of Liability

Consultant's total liability under this Agreement will not exceed the total fees paid in the 3 months preceding the claim.

Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

---

## 9. Warranties

Consultant warrants that: (a) the services will be performed in a professional manner; (b) the deliverables will not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights; (c) Consultant has the right to enter into this Agreement.

Consultant does not warrant that deliverables will be error-free or that AI tools used in the engagement will produce specific results.

---

## 10. Independent Contractor

Consultant is an independent contractor. Client is not responsible for taxes, benefits, or insurance on Consultant's behalf. Consultant will maintain any business registrations and pay applicable taxes.

---

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [Province, Canada / State, USA]. Disputes will be resolved in the courts of [jurisdiction], or by arbitration if both parties agree.

---

## 12. Entire Agreement

This Agreement (including all SOWs) constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

**Checklist before sending to a real client:**
- [ ] Have a lawyer review (especially IP, limitation of liability, and governing law clauses)
- [ ] Register a business name or incorporate if engaging as a company rather than as an individual
- [ ] Get liability insurance (Errors & Omissions / Professional Liability) — often required by enterprise clients
- [ ] Confirm HST/GST registration if billing Canadian clients above the registration threshold
- [ ] Consider an NDA as a separate document if the client prefers their own form
