# Cold Email Templates

For reaching out to people you don't know via email. Lower conversion than warm LinkedIn outreach — use sparingly and only when you have a specific, relevant hook.

---

## Structure

- Subject: specific, not clever
- Body: 3–4 sentences max
- Ask: one thing only

---

## Template A — Problem-first

**Subject:** Design system + AI tooling — quick question

> Hi [Name],
>
> Saw [Company] is using Figma [or: has a design system, or: posted about AI tooling] — we ran into a specific problem on similar setups that I haven't seen many teams talk about yet: AI-generated components that look right but break the token contract and don't exist in Storybook.
>
> Built something that addresses it. Worth 20 minutes?
>
> Jacques

---

## Template B — Referral/warm intro

**Subject:** [Mutual contact] suggested I reach out

> Hi [Name],
>
> [Mutual] mentioned you're dealing with [specific problem] at [Company]. It's something I've been working on directly — built an agentic layer for a design team that catches the gaps between Figma AI output and the actual component system before it hits the codebase.
>
> Would a short call be useful?
>
> Jacques

---

## Template C — After they publish something relevant

**Subject:** Re: your post on [topic]

> Hi [Name],
>
> Read your [article / talk / post] on [topic] — the part about [specific thing] matched something I ran into directly while building [brief reference]. I've been working on infrastructure that addresses exactly that gap.
>
> Would you be open to a 20-minute call? Happy to share what we built and get your reaction.
>
> Jacques

---

## Notes

- Find emails via LinkedIn, company website, or Apollo/Hunter
- Verify deliverability before sending to a list
- Track replies — if a template isn't converting after 20 sends, it's the message, not the list
- Personalization beats volume every time at this price point
