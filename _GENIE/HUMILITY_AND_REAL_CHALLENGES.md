## The Message It Sends

> “I built something good. It works. But adoption is harder than I expected. Teams are tired. People resist change for legitimate reasons. I’m learning to listen better, to be more patient, to respect when people say no. And I’m learning that real impact isn’t about being right—it’s about understanding why people resist and building trust over time.”

This is **so much more powerful** than:

Because the first one is **true**. And people can feel when something is true.
